Maximal margin classifier intuition: maximize the gap between classes at the decision boundary. Support vectors as the critical points. Kernel trick: transforms data to a higher-dimensional space without computing coordinates explicitly. RBF kernel: effective when decision boundary is nonlinear; gamma controls locality.
