# DATA 322 AI Assistant MVP

## Goal

Build a course-aware AI assistant that supports students while preserving instructor control, learning integrity, and privacy boundaries.

## MVP Features

- Course context ingestion
- Per-student tutoring chat
- Assignment-aware help modes
- Step-by-step explanation
- Quiz and self-check prompts
- Misconception tracking
- Instructor-facing summary of common confusion
- Teacher controls and safety boundaries

## Student Help Modes

- Explain the concept more simply.
- Give another example.
- Use an analogy.
- Quiz me.
- Show step by step.
- Help me find where I got confused.
- Help me plan the assignment without doing it for me.

## Instructor Dashboard Possibilities

- Confusion clusters
- Common misconceptions
- Topics needing reteach
- Quiz performance trends
- Engagement patterns
- Frequently asked questions

## Boundaries

- Do not silently complete graded work for students.
- Do not expose one student's private data to another student.
- Do not treat AI output as authoritative without verification.
- Keep the instructor in charge of course policy.
