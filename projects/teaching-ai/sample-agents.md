# Sample Agents

Sample agents should make AI capabilities concrete for teaching and workshops.

## Candidate Agents

Research assistant:

Helps gather, compare, summarize, and cite information.

Tutor:

Explains concepts at different levels, gives examples, checks understanding, and adapts to confusion.

Coding helper:

Turns a small idea into a plan, scaffolds code, explains errors, and suggests tests.

Workflow planner:

Breaks a messy task into steps, dependencies, artifacts, and follow-ups.

Socratic questioner:

Asks questions that help learners reason rather than simply receiving answers.

Verifier:

Checks claims, identifies weak assumptions, and suggests evidence needed.
