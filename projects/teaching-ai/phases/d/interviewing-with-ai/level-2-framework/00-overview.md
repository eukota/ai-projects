# Level 2: Perform Well in Interviews with AI

Teaching framework for demonstrating AI competency in technical interviews.

## What This Course Does

You've learned Steve Yegge's 8 stages of AI developer progression (Level 1). Now you'll learn how to **show that mastery in an interview setting**.

This course teaches you to:
- Demonstrate each skill the interviewer is evaluating
- Communicate your thinking clearly
- Handle interview constraints (time, pressure, live coding)
- Move from Entry → Mid → Senior level performance

## How It Works

**1. The Rubric (Your Evaluation Criteria)**

Your interviewer uses this rubric to evaluate you:
- AI Awareness/Experience
- Code Proficiency
- AI Proficiency
- Requirements gathering
- Design & Implementation
- Testing

You need to understand what each level looks like so you can hit Mid or Senior.

**2. The Interview Flow**

A typical AI-assisted interview follows this pattern:
1. AI Awareness discussion (upfront)
2. Requirements gathering
3. Design & Implementation
4. Testing
5. Discussion/wrap-up

We'll teach you how to perform at each stage.

**3. Mock Interviews**

You'll do mock interviews with real feedback against the rubric. This is where it gets real.

## Course Structure

- **AI Awareness in Interviews** — How to establish credibility and signal your level
- **Requirements Phase** — How to ask good questions and scope work
- **Design & Implementation Phase** — How to plan, design, and code with AI
- **Testing Phase** — How to validate your solution
- **Communication & Articulation** — How to think out loud and explain decisions
- **Mock Interview Structure** — What to expect, how to prepare
- **Self-Assessment** — Using the rubric to identify your gaps

## Key Insight

**The interview is not a test of your AI skills. It's a test of how well you can demonstrate and communicate your AI skills.**

Entry-level candidates know how to code but can't explain their AI usage. Mid-level candidates can explain it. Senior candidates can teach it.

This course teaches you to move up that ladder.
