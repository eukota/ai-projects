# AI-Unlocked Individuals

An AI-unlocked individual understands how to use AI as a leverage system.

## Core Capabilities

- Ask better questions.
- Break ambiguous work into AI-assistable tasks.
- Use AI for learning, creation, research, coding, and execution.
- Verify outputs instead of blindly trusting them.
- Combine AI with domain judgment.
- Build workflows rather than rely only on one-off prompts.
- Understand when local, private, or frontier systems are appropriate.

## Growth Mindset vs Fear Mindset

People experimenting now will compound capability quickly.

The teaching stance should be practical and empowering: AI is a tool, collaborator, and amplifier, not a replacement for judgment.

## Recursive Acceleration

AI helps people learn AI faster. That lets them build better systems, which then helps them learn and execute faster again.
