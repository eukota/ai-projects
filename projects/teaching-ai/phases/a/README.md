# Phase A: Foundation

**Goal**: Help people see AI as opportunity, not threat. Acknowledge concerns. Establish why learning AI matters.

**Who this is for**: Anyone new to or hesitant about AI. People overcoming fear or skepticism.

## Level 0: Overcoming Fear & Building Motivation

- [Vision](level-0/vision.md) — Mission, identity, central beliefs about AI as force multiplier
- [Risks](level-0/risks.md) — Honest discussion of hallucinations, cheating, dependency, privacy, job loss + responses

## Key Themes

- Practical, non-hype approach
- Acknowledges real concerns  
- Focuses on capability building, not replacement
- Safe, empowering, hands-on framing
