# Teaching AI Vision

## Mission

Create more AI-unlocked individuals.

The goal is not AI hype or replacing humans. The goal is helping people become dramatically more capable by learning how to work with AI.

AI literacy is becoming comparable to learning web search in the Google era, spreadsheets in the office era, or programming in the software era.

## Identity Statement

I help people become AI-capable by turning powerful but confusing technology into practical systems they can actually use.

## Central Beliefs

AI is a force multiplier:

A motivated person using AI can accelerate learning, creation, research, coding, and execution far beyond a traditional pace.

Most people are underusing it:

Many people are avoiding AI, afraid of it, using it superficially, or unaware of what is possible.

Prompting is only layer one:

The deeper stack includes tool use, memory and context systems, agents, multi-agent orchestration, domain workflows, and human plus AI systems design.

Education is vulnerable to disruption:

Traditional education assumes scarce knowledge, fixed pacing, one teacher to many students, and delayed feedback. AI changes all of those.
