# AI as a System
## From Tools to Agents to Orchestration

Audience: Cal Poly Humboldt Colloquium  
Speaker: Darrell Ross

---

# Slide 1: Opening Question

How many people here have used AI this week?

How many feel confident they understand where this is going?

---

# Slide 2: Why This Matters

We are entering a shift comparable to:

- Search engines
- Smartphones
- Cloud computing

But faster.

AI is becoming a general-purpose amplifier for human capability.

---

# Slide 3: The New Literacy

Old phrase:

> Let me Google that for you.

New phrase:

> Let me ask AI for you.

Knowing how to work with AI is becoming a core skill.

---

# Slide 4: AI Is More Than Chat

AI can function as:

- Tutor
- Research assistant
- Programmer
- Editor
- Analyst
- Brainstorming partner
- Agent worker

---

# Slide 5: Layers of Capability

## Level 1: Prompting
Ask better questions.

## Level 2: Structured Outputs
Reliable machine-readable results.

## Level 3: Tool Use
AI calls software tools.

## Level 4: Agents
AI performs multi-step tasks.

## Level 5: Orchestration
Multiple agents collaborate.
