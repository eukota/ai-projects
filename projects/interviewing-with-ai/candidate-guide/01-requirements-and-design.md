# Requirements & Design

Gathering requirements and designing the solution with AI.

**Timeline:** 10-15 minutes total (5 min prompt + response + iteration)

## The Approach

- Type focused prompt directly into AI chat (signal: moving fast)
- Clear statement of design goal
- Name the AI process/tool you want ("use brainstorming," "generate a plan," etc.)
- List requirements/constraints
- Ask interviewer clarifying questions in real-time and encode answers into constraints
- AI responds
- You read and think critically
- Call out what you don't want, set priorities to show you're in control
- Work with AI toward the plan through back-and-forth iteration (collaborative refinement)
- Result: refined plan you've built together

## Interview Signaling

- **Opening move:** "AI could spend 4 hours designing, but this is an interview, so I'm jumping in by giving requirements and getting a quick plan"
- **As you read:** Speak out loud about what you're gaining from the AI response

## Advanced Strategies (require skill; can backfire)

### 1. Bring your own design process

Apply your known process with AI, but compress it for interview time

- **Risk:** spending 30 minutes on something that could be 5 minutes

### 2. Spawn background AI research

Run a parallel process to research best practices while you work

- **Must:** circle back and actually incorporate the results
- **Anti-pattern:** forgetting to return to the response (looks showy but scattered)

### 3. Devote more time to the plan

Spend additional time building a very solid, detailed plan

- **Only on your own local system:** where you know exactly how it will perform
- **Only if:** you have lots of experience and know your system can implement quickly with a good plan
- **Bet:** a solid plan enables fast, clean implementation
- **Risk:** if you run out of time, interviewer sees miscalculation (credibility hit)
- **Payoff:** if you pull it off, interviewer sees confidence and execution ability (credibility boost)
