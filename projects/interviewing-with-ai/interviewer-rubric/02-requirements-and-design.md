# Requirements & Design

How candidates gather requirements and design solutions with AI.

Observe during the Requirements & Design phase of the interview. Look for how well they apply core principles.

## Observable Signals

### Active Direction
*Does the candidate direct the AI, or passively accept?*

**Low:**
- Accepts AI's first output without pushback
- Doesn't ask follow-up questions
- Seems unsure about what they want

**Medium:**
- Asks AI to adjust or refine output
- Makes some requests for changes
- Iterates a bit, but doesn't push hard

**High:**
- Clearly states what they want/don't want
- Pushes back on AI suggestions
- Directs AI toward their vision iteratively

---

### Articulation & Narrative Control
*Does the candidate think out loud and guide the scope?*

**Low:**
- Silent while reading AI output
- Doesn't explain reasoning to interviewer
- Doesn't ask constraining questions

**Medium:**
- Explains some of their thinking
- Asks interviewer clarifying questions
- Attempts to narrow scope

**High:**
- Consistently articulates what they're evaluating
- Asks strategic questions to constrain the problem
- Clearly explains design choices and priorities

---

### Collaboration
*Are they working WITH the AI or outsourcing?*

**Low:**
- Uses AI as a black box
- Doesn't engage with the output
- Delegates all thinking to AI

**Medium:**
- Reads AI output carefully
- Engages with proposals
- Makes decisions based on AI suggestions

**High:**
- Actively shapes AI output
- Treats AI as a thinking partner
- Makes decisions independently, informed by AI

---

### Explains & Compares
*Do they ask AI to clarify unfamiliar concepts?*

**Low:**
- Doesn't ask for explanation
- Moves forward with gaps in understanding
- Assumes understanding without verifying

**Medium:**
- Asks AI to explain something once or twice
- May not ask for comparisons to familiar concepts

**High:**
- Asks AI to explain unclear concepts
- Requests comparisons to things they know
- Uses explanations to build mental model

---

## Scoring

**Low:** Passive, doesn't direct, accepts without pushback  
**Medium:** Engages with AI, iterates, asks questions  
**High:** Actively directs, articulates thinking, collaborates strategically
