# AI Experience

Candidate's background, knowledge, and track record with AI.

Assess during early interview discussion. Listen for claims about tools, models, projects, and controls they've mastered.

## Evaluation Areas

### AI Landscape
*Knowledge of tools, models, and current capabilities*

**Low:**
- Only knows one tool (ChatGPT) or only chat mode
- Can't name models or explain differences
- No awareness of tool strengths/weaknesses

**Medium:**
- Knows 2-3 tools/models (Claude, ChatGPT, etc.)
- Can explain basic differences (speed vs. reasoning)
- Has opinions on when to use what

**High:**
- Deep knowledge of multiple tools and models
- Understands tradeoffs (context, speed, reasoning, cost)
- Aware of emerging patterns and capabilities

---

### AI Journey
*Understanding of AI evolution and capability inflection points*

**Low:**
- Thinks AI capability is static
- No awareness of recent changes (Dec 2025 jump)
- Hasn't noticed or thought about trajectory

**Medium:**
- Aware capability has improved over time
- Can point to recent capability shifts
- Understands this affects approach/strategy

**High:**
- Deep understanding of AI timeline and inflection points
- Conscious of Dec 2025+ capability jump
- Actively adapting strategy as capabilities evolve

---

### AI Agency
*Has the candidate actually built and shared?*

**Low:**
- Only uses AI reactively (chat mode)
- No projects built with AI
- Hasn't experimented or shared with others

**Medium:**
- Has built a few AI projects (personal or work)
- Experimented outside of work
- May have shared or helped a coworker

**High:**
- Multiple projects built, both personal and work
- Actively experimenting and iterating
- Teaching/helping others learn AI
- Building AI-based tools for reuse

---

### AI Controls
*Knowledge of AI primitives: skills, rules, agents, orchestrators*

**Low:**
- Only knows basic prompting
- Can't explain or use skills, rules, agents
- Thinks AI is a single interaction model

**Medium:**
- Aware of skills, rules, agents as concepts
- Has used one or more in practice
- Understands when to apply each

**High:**
- Proficient with multiple AI primitives
- Has built custom skills, rules, or agents
- Orchestrates them strategically
- Can explain when/why each control is useful

---

## Scoring

Assess the candidate across all four areas. Look for:
- **Consistency:** Do their claims match demonstrated knowledge?
- **Depth:** How deep is their understanding, not just breadth?
- **Growth:** Are they still learning and adapting?

**Low overall:** Needs foundational AI work before advanced interviews  
**Medium overall:** Solid foundation; ready for solution execution  
**High overall:** Strong AI practitioner with clear growth trajectory
